design and development of learning management system at universiti putra malaysia : a case study of e-sprint this paper reports the design and development of the e-sprint , learning management system , which has been derived from sistem pengurusan rangkaian integrasi notakuliah dalam talian - mod elektronik ) and currently being implemented at universiti putra malaysia ( upm ) . the e-sprint was developed by utilizing perl ( practical extraction and report language ) and was supported by standard database in linux\/unix environment operating system . the system is currently being used to supplement and complement part of the classroom-based teaching . this paper covers the architecture and features of the e-sprint system which consists of five main modules . some general issues and challenges of such e-learning initiatives implementation will also be discussed .
