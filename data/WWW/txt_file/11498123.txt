on business activity modeling using grammars web based applications offer a mainstream channel for businesses to manage their activities . we model such business activity in a grammar-based framework . the backus naur form notation is used to represent the syntax of a regular grammar corresponding to web log patterns of interest . then , a deterministic finite state machine is used to parse web logs against the grammar . detected tasks are associated with metadata such as time taken to perform the activity , and aggregated along relevant corporate dimensions .
