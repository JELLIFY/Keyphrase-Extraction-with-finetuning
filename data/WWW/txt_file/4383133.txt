a content and structure website mining model we present a novel model for validating and improving the content and structure organization of a website . this model studies the website as a graph and evaluates its interconnectivity in relation to the similarity of its documents . the aim of this model is to provide a simple way for improving the overall structure , contents and interconnectivity of a website . this model has been implemented as a prototype and applied to several websites , showing very interesting results . our model is complementary to other methods of website personalization and improvement .
