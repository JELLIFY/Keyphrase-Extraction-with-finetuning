searching the workplace web the social impact from the world wide web can not be underestimated , but technologies used to build the web are also revolutionizing the sharing of business and government information within intranets . in many ways the lessons learned from the internet carry over directly to intranets , but others do not apply . in particular , the social forces that guide the development of intranets are quite different , and the determination of a `` good answer '' for intranet search is quite different than on the internet . in this paper we study the problem of intranet search . our approach focuses on the use of rank aggregation , and allows us to examine the effects of different heuristics on ranking of search results .
